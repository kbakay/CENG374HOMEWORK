Parrot Runs Away

15.09.2023 - 30.06.2024 Tayyare Culture Center

Play Summary: Armağan is a parrot who lives with his grandparents, Rüstem and Gül. Feeling bored with his monotonous daily routine, Armağan decides to run away from home one day, lured by the deceptive words of Yellow Crow. Meanwhile, Yellow Crow schemes to take Armağan's place in the household. As Armağan wanders the streets, he encounters numerous challenges and experiences a series of events. However, during this journey, he savors the adventure of venturing out on his own and tasting freedom. Along the way, he makes new friends and discovers his own strength. Through this adventurous journey, Armağan learns new things about himself and his surroundings, and he matures. Ultimately, as he returns home, he realizes that the true solution to escaping the monotony of life lies within himself.
