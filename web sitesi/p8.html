RED WALL (CHIELD PLAY)

08.11.2023 - 31.05.2024 Tayyare Culture Center

THE RED WALL The Great Red Wall had always been there. But the little Mouse's friends didn't know why the wall was there. They didn't know what lay beyond the wall either. All they knew was that they were very, very afraid of the wall. Would the little Mouse find the courage to discover what lay beyond? Could it take that first step into the world where freedom and possibilities awaited? Children's Play - Ages 4-6
