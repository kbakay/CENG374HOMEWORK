"The Wedding of Flowers" Silk Needle Lace Exhibition

11.04.2023 - 04.05.2024 Merinos Textile Industry Museum

The exhibition titled "Wedding of Flowers" will be on display at the Merinos Textile Industry Museum starting from May 4, 2023, coinciding with the opening of the Needle Lace Festival. Needle lace art, also known as Turkish lace, is a unique Turkish handicraft that, despite being highly admired, is not widely known for its production process. In Anatolia, when people were silenced or forced to remain silent, the colors and motifs they chose in their needle laceworks spoke volumes. Green symbolized desires, blue represented hopes, white signified happiness, while black depicted grief. Moreover, lace adorned with purple lilacs expressed those who had fallen in love, while hopeless love and heartaches found their expression in lace designs. Named after the most beautiful season of the year, spring, and its harbinger, flowers, the exhibition "Wedding of Flowers" is meticulously curated with inspiration from nature's latest creations. It presents a selection of hundreds of needle lace pieces crafted by the skilled hands of Anatolian people, pouring from their silent inner gardens. The exhibition features collections from Mr. Ikumi Nonaka and Mr. İbrahim Koca. Date: May 4, 2023 - May 4, 2024 Time: 14:00 Location: Merinos Textile Industry Museum
