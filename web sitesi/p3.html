"The 100-Year Industrial Heritage of the Republic; 'Century Together Bursa' Exhibition"

Exhibition 15.02.2024 - 15.05.2024 Merinos Textile Industry Museum

In collaboration with Siemens, we have opened the exhibition "The 100-Year Industrial Heritage of the Republic; Together for a Century" to commemorate the 86th anniversary of Sümerbank Merinos Industrial Factory, one of the most valuable industrial heritages witnessing Turkey's history from its establishment to the present day, at Merinos Textile Industry Museum, Turkey's first textile industry museum. The exhibition, which narrates the founding story of our 100-year-old Republic through the history of Sümerbank Merinos Factory and other industrial institutions, as well as through education and real human stories, will unveil objects and documents from the collections of Sümerbank Merinos Factory that have not been exhibited before. The "The 100-Year Industrial Heritage of the Republic; Together for a Century Bursa" Exhibition will be open for free visitation from February 15th to May 15th, between 09:00 and 17:30. Reflecting the century-long technological partnership between the Republic of Turkey and Siemens Turkey, the exhibition is curated by Prof. Dr. Önder Küçükerman. We look forward to your participation in "The 100-Year Industrial Heritage of the Republic; Together for a Century" Exhibition.
