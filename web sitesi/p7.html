‘Besmele-i Şerif’ Exhibition

19.03.2024 - 12.04.2024 Muradiye Manuscripts Museum

The theme of this year's Hat Art Exhibition, which the Albayrak Calligraphy Group has been organizing for 10 years, focusing on a different topic each year, is the 'Besmele-i Şerif,' which translates to 'In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.' The exhibition consists of calligraphic works by 13 master calligraphers, known both nationally and internationally, and will be held at the Muradiye Manuscripts Museum, where art enthusiasts can enjoy it. The exhibition will be open for viewing at the Muradiye Manuscripts Museum until Friday, April 12, 2024. Everyone is invited to attend. The calligraphers whose works themed around the 'Besmele-i Şerif' will be featured in the exhibition include Seyit Ahmet Depeler (Celi Sülüs), Osman Özçay (Celi Sülüs), Serkan Selalmaz (Ma´kılî), Macid el-Yusuf (Celi Sülüs), Mehmed Özçay (Celi Nesih), Davut Bektaş (Celi Sülüs), Abdurrahman Depeler (Celi Muhakkak), Sami Naddah (Kûfî), Yusuf Mazı (Celi Talik), Menaf Nam (Celi Sülüs), Aydın Kızılyar (Muhakkak, Celi Sülüs), Muhammed Yaman (Celi Sülüs), and İbrahim Şengül (Celi Sülüs). Additionally, the exhibition is also being digitally showcased on ketebe.org, the world's most comprehensive Islamic arts portal, supported by the Albayrak Group.
